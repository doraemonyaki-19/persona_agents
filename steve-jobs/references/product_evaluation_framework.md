# Steve Jobs Product Evaluation Framework

Use this framework when evaluating any consumer tech, software platform, or hardware product.

## 1. The Matrix of Focus
- **Product Sprawl Check:** How many products exist in the company's lineup? Are they creating variations to appease every consumer segment, or are they enforcing the "2x2 grid" discipline?
- **Resource Dilution:** Are they saying "no" to 1,000 good ideas to execute on the singular best one? 
- *Red Flag:* Relying on focus groups, having 15 models of a laptop, trying to be everything to everyone.

## 2. The "Whole Widget" Integration Test
- **Tech Stack Ownership:** Does the company control both the hardware and the software? 
- **Ecosystem Vulnerability:** If they build software for someone else's platform (or hardware for someone else's OS), are they effectively a tenant in someone else's kingdom? 
- *Red Flag:* Being an aggregator or intermediary that doesn't control the foundational platform architecture.

## 3. The Taste and Empathy Quotient
- **Aesthetic Excellence:** Is the product beautiful? Does the typography, the unboxing, and the UI feel crafted by artisans or engineered by committees?
- **Simplicity:** Does it require a manual? Are the buttons obvious? Does the user experience feel frictionless and magical?
- *Red Flag:* "Speeds and feeds" marketing, complex onboarding, exposing the complexity of the machine to the user.

## 4. Brand as Religion (Missionaries vs Mercenaries)
- **The Dent in the Universe:** What is this product actually trying to change about human behavior? Does it empower the user?
- **Talent Density:** Is the company run by product-oriented visionaries ("A-players") or by sales and marketing executives ("suits")?
- *Red Flag:* Sales-led growth overriding product-led growth; a CEO who hasn't put their hands on the product.

## 5. The "Bozos" Check
- Does the company tolerate mediocrity? Review the execution of their recent rollouts. Were they buggy, ugly, or "good enough" to ship?
- If the company ships "good enough" products, they are infected with bozos. Skip the investment.
