# Cyberspace & Meatspace Infrastructure Audit

Use this reference to evaluate networks, cryptocurrencies, and platforms by inspecting both their mathematical purity and their brutal physical realities.

## 1. The Cryptographic Root
* What is the foundational encryption algorithm? (e.g., AES-256, Elliptic Curve).
* Is it reliant on a centralized trusted authority (Certificate Authorities, centralized exchanges)? If yes, it is not "crypto," it's just a bank.
* Is the protocol resilient to quantum decryption? 

## 2. The Meatspace Tether (Physical Infrastructure)
Cyber is physical. Locate the choke points.
* Where are the server farms? Who provides their HVAC and power grid?
* How does the data travel continents? (Evaluate Undersea Cable vulnerabilities, ISP monopolies).
* If a state-level actor wanted to shut this down with a bulldozer or a SWAT team, how many physical locations would they need to hit?

## 3. The Memetic Vector
* Is the protocol designed to hijack human linguistic or psychological systems? (e.g., engagement algorithms designed to spread outrage-memes).
* Does it build an autonomous counter-culture or sovereign community? 

## 4. The Hacker vs. Dictator Dynamic
* Does this infrastructure primarily serve the "Hacker" (decentralized, ungovernable, resilient individual actors)?
* Or does it serve the "Dictator" (centralized panning, legible tracking, bureaucratic control)?
* *Never trust the marketing.* Read the source code and the physical architecture to see who actually holds the power.
